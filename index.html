Short, practical tips

How to improve fast

  • Track your losses: was it matchup, misplay, or overcommit?
  • Learn 1 deck deeply before switching.
  • Count elixir & predict cycles.

Deck basics

  • Have a win condition + support + defense + spells.
  • Don’t stack too expensive cards (keep it balanced).
  • Include at least one reset / control option if needed.
